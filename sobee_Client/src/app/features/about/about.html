How It All Started

It was 2020, and like many, we found ourselves searching for something better. Better ways to stay focused, better ingredients in our drinks, better choices for our bodies. The energy drink aisle was full of options, but none that truly aligned with what we wanted.

So we decided to create it ourselves. After months of research, countless taste tests, and rigorous quality checks, SoBee was born. A drink that delivers real energy from real ingredients, without the artificial junk you find in typical energy drinks.

Today, SoBee is more than just a drink - it's a movement. A community of people who refuse to settle for less when it comes to what they put in their bodies. And we're just getting started.
